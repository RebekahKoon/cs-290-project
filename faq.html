Frequently Asked Questions

What Are Your Hours?

Our grooming salon is open Monday through Thursday 9am - 5pm, Friday 9am - 2pm, and Saturday and Sunday 9am - 12pm.

How Long Will it Take to Groom My Pet?

The time needed to groom your pet varies depending on the size of your dog and the type of your appointment. A bath will take approximately one hour, a bath and grooming will take about an hour and a half, and a bath, grooming, and styled cut appointment will take up to two hours.

What Type of Shampoo is Used?

We use Earthbath shampoo when bathing your dog. This is a high-quality, all-natural oatmeal and aloe shampoo that is often used for dogs with sensitive skin. If your dog has any allergies to this shampoo, please let us know so that we can use an alternative shampoo.

What Dog Breeds Can You Groom?

We are experienced in grooming several different types of dogs. Please let us know what breed your dog is and we can groom your pet accordingly!

How Often Should I Groom My Dog?

This often depends on the breed of your dog. Long-haired dogs typically need to be bathed and groomed approximately every 1 to 2 months, whereas short-haired dogs can go about 3 months without being groomed. However, dogs should be brushed at least once per week, regardless of their breed. More information can be found here.

Can You Groom My Cat?

Although we primarily groom dogs, we are also able to groom cats. Grooming for cats includes brushing their fur, clipping their nails, and cleaning their ears. However, we are unable to give your cat a bath. Please be aware that your cat will likely be around dogs while being groomed.
