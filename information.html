Grooming Information

Below are the types of services we can provide for your dog. Unless otherwise requested, dogs are bathed in shampoo for sensitive skin. Dogs are also blow-dried using cool air. Please let us know if your pet needs any special accommodations.

Basic Bath

  • Cost: $30
  • Bath and shampoo
  • Blow-dry
  • Brushed with a slicker brush
  • Not available for cats

Bath and Grooming

  • Cost: $45
  • Bath and shampoo
  • Blow-dry
  • Brushed with a slicker brush
  • Nail clipping
  • Ear cleaning
  • Available for cats. Bath not included.

Styled Cut

  • Cost: $60
  • Bath and shampoo
  • Blow-dry
  • Brushed with a slicker brush
  • Nail clipping
  • Ear cleaning
  • Styled cut for your dog's breed
  • Not availabe for cats
